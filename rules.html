Server Rules

Please read and follow the rules to keep the community safe.

1. No Hacks or Cheats

The use of modified clients, x-ray, duping, or any unfair advantages is strictly prohibited and will result in a permanent ban.

2. No Griefing

Do not destroy or alter another player's build without their permission. Respect protected areas and claims.

3. Be Respectful

No racism, sexism, homophobia, harassment, or excessive toxicity in chat. Treat all players and staff with respect.

4. No Advertising

Do not advertise other Minecraft servers, Discord servers, or personal links without staff permission.
